About Me

Hi! I’m Aly, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer with a strong eye for clean, functional, and visually engaging designs. My journey in tech started with curiosity and grew into a drive to design purposeful interfaces and bring them to life through code. Beyond design tools and development, I value collaboration, innovation, and the process of continuous learning — creating not just projects, but meaningful digital experiences.
